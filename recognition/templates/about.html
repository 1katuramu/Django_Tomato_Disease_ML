Our Mission

At TomatoHealth Detector, we're committed to revolutionizing tomato farming through cutting-edge AI technology. Our mission is to help farmers identify tomato diseases efficiently, leading to healthier crops and increased yields.

The Technology

Our TomatoHealth Detector employs advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze images of tomato fruits. By leveraging deep learning techniques, we can accurately identify whether tomatoes are ripe, unripe, or reject quality.

The model has been trained on a diverse dataset of tomato images, covering various stages of ripeness and quality levels. This enables our system to deliver quick and accurate classifications, empowering farmers to make informed decisions promptly.

How It Works

  1. Upload an image of your tomato plant or fruit.
  2. Our AI model analyzes the image in real-time.
  3. Receive an instant diagnosis of the tomato health.
